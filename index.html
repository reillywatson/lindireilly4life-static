Wedding Events

Aside from a wedding ceremony, there are also some other things happening! Here's a rough outline of what to expect in terms of food and events.

Snacks and drinks

All weekend We plan on having several kegs of tasty beer, as well as wine and spirits the likes of which you can't even conceive. Snackwise, probably basic party stuff like chips and hummus and dip and what have you. You can conceive of the likes of those.

General Friday Night Shenanigans

Friday, 7pm - late Come anytime after 7, but don't expect us to feed you! I mean we'll have snacks, but like Lindi always says, "Reilly, chips are not for supper".

Saturday Breakfast

Saturday, morning times Breakfast will be pretty casual stuff, like cereal, toast, fruit, croissants, that sort of thing. Buffet-style, eat when you are awake and hungry. Do not attempt while asleep and full.

Saturday Lunch

Saturday, noonish Lunch will be a barbecue. The fabulous Fred Pruss has offered his famous burger-flipping skills. Expect things like hamburgers, hot dogs, veggie burgers, veggie sausages, and salads.

Wedding Ceremony

Saturday, 2:30pm-3:00pm We're totally getting married during this time. It will be outside, and we have a tent if it rains, which it won't.

"Cocktail Hour"

Saturday, 3:00pm-6:00pm Not really an hour! This is secretly three hours, in which there will be some sweet hanging out. There will be games; perhaps people will play them. Perhaps the games will play people.

Supper

Saturday, 6:00pm-8:00pm Supper will be gourmet wood-fired pizza, cooked on site by these guys. For dessert there will be delicious cupcakes.

Speeches

Saturday, 7:00pm Wherein the same people who cried at the ceremony cry some more.

Dancin'!

Saturday, 8:00pm-late The tent area will turn into a dance party. We can pretend it's spontaneous if you want, but it's definitely been planned!

Campfire

Saturday, late Fire's burning, fire's burning, draw near, draw near, etc.

Sunday Breakfast

Sunday, morning times Probably similar to Saturday breakfast!

Go Home

Sunday, sometime Party's over man!

Location

The wedding is taking place at Breathe Easy cottage, about half an hour's drive south of Owen Sound. It's on a fairly large property (140 acres), so there should be plenty of space. Some people will have to sleep in tents, so if you're into that please say so! Lindi and Reilly will be sleeping in a Gypsy caravan, as per tradition.

The address is 214838 Baseline Road, Grey County, N0G 1R0. Google Maps can show you where to go, but failing that here are some directions to get there from Toronto:

Take the 401 west to 410 north, past Brampton, then over to hwy10 north, past Orangeville, Shelburne (right turn in centre of town), Flesherton, then to Markdale. Turn left at the stoplights onto Grey Road 12, go to the next paved highway (hwy6, about 12miles) and turn right.

Go north about 4 miles to Dornoch then turn left on Grey Road 25 (paved). Go west 2 miles then turn left onto Baseline Rd which begins there (gravel). Drive south a mile or so, past Concession 12 and watch for the driveway on the right with the little green sign "214838".
